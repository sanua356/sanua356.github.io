Alan Walker

Is a British-born Norwegian DJ and record producer. He is best known for his 2015 single "Faded" which received diamond certifications in Germany and Poland and multi-platinum certifications in over 10 countries including the US and the UK. He released his debut studio album, Different World, in 2018. He was ranked 36th on DJ Mag's Top 100 DJs list of 2018.

About me

My Life

British-born Norwegian DJ and record producer.

At the age of two, moved to Bergen, Norway, with his parents and siblings. Grew up with two siblings, an older sister. Inspired by the producers of EDM K-391 and Ahrix, as well as composers such as Hans Zimmer and Steve Jablonski, I started releasing my music on my laptop using FL Studio.

In July 2012, thanks to feedback from his fans on the Internet, he began his music career and slowly began publishing his music on YouTube and SoundCloud.
